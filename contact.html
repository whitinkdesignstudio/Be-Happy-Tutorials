Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our programs.

Is the Intro Talk really free? ▾
Yes, completely free with no obligations. We hold it every Saturday at 10 AM. Simply register via the contact form or WhatsApp us and we'll confirm your seat.
Do I need prior experience? ▾
No prior experience is needed for the Intro Talk or Sky Breath Workshop. These programs are designed to be completely beginner-friendly. Just bring an open mind.
How are the programs priced? ▾
The Intro Talk is free. For paid programs, please contact us directly for current pricing. We offer concessions for students and those in financial need.
Can I attend online? ▾
We offer online versions of the Intro Talk and Morning Breathwork Circle. The Sky Breath Workshop, Sahaj Samadhi, and Silent Retreat are best experienced in person.
Do you offer corporate wellness programs? ▾
Yes! We work with companies to design customised stress management and wellness workshops for teams. Mention "Corporate" in your message.
