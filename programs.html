Justice System

Prison SMART Program

Prison SMART (Stress Management and Rehabilitation Training) is IAHV's unique rehabilitation program for inmates. Its mission is to make a life-transforming difference in the criminal justice system by teaching skills for reducing stress, healing trauma, and providing practical knowledge of how to handle emotions and live to one's highest potential. Started 1992 in the US — now implemented in over 60 countries, benefiting more than 400,000 people worldwide.

5 Days
Correctional facilities
  • Reduces stress, aggression and trauma
  • Practical tools to manage emotions and live to highest potential
  • Aims to reduce recidivism and break the cycle of re-offending
  • Implemented in 60+ countries; 400,000+ beneficiaries
  • Teaches non-violence, creativity and positive human qualities
