About Be Happy

Our mission is to make authentic wellness practices accessible to everyone, creating a world where inner peace is a way of life.

Our Story

Born from a Simple Belief

Be Happy was founded with one simple belief: that every human being deserves to experience joy, calm, and clarity. Inspired by the transformative techniques of the Art of Living foundation, we set out to build a community where ancient wisdom meets modern life.

What started as a small group gathering in Ahmedabad has grown into a thriving wellness community of hundreds of members across Gujarat, united by the shared experience of breathwork and meditation.

Our Mission

Bringing Peace to Every Home

We believe that a peaceful world begins with peaceful individuals. Our mission is to equip every person with practical tools — breathing techniques, meditation practices, and community support — to navigate life's challenges with grace.

Every program we offer is rooted in time-tested techniques that have been validated by science and experienced by millions worldwide.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Be Happy.

🌱

Authenticity

We teach only what we practice ourselves, staying true to ancient wisdom that has guided millions toward inner freedom.

🤝

Community

Wellness is not a solo journey. We foster genuine connections and build a family of practitioners who uplift each other.

🔬

Science-Backed

Our practices are validated by modern research, bridging ancient techniques with contemporary understanding of mind-body health.

💚

Inclusivity

Wellness is for everyone, regardless of age, background, or prior experience. We welcome all seekers with open arms.

🎯

Transformation

We are committed to lasting change. Our programs are designed to create sustainable shifts in how you live.

🕊️

Service

Giving back is at our core. We regularly offer free sessions and support underserved communities to access wellness.
