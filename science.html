Key Research Findings

A selection of peer-reviewed studies on the practices we teach.

Breathwork

Sudarshan Kriya and the Autonomic Nervous System

Studies found that Sudarshan Kriya creates measurable shifts in autonomic nervous system balance, moving practitioners from sympathetic (fight-or-flight) to parasympathetic (rest-and-digest) dominance.

Journal of Alternative & Complementary Medicine, 2005
Mental Health

Meditation and Anxiety Reduction

A meta-analysis of 47 trials with over 3,500 participants found that mindfulness meditation programmes showed moderate evidence of improving anxiety, depression, and pain.

JAMA Internal Medicine, 2014
Neuroscience

Meditation and Brain Structure

Harvard Medical School research showed that regular meditators have measurably thicker cortical regions associated with attention, interoception, and sensory processing.

NeuroReport, 2005
Hormones

Reduced Cortisol and Improved Wellbeing

Research on Art of Living programs found significant reductions in blood cortisol and ACTH levels, alongside improvements in reported wellbeing and emotional regulation.

Psychoneuroendocrinology, 2012
